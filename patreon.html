ONBOARDING FLOW // WHAT HAPPENS WHEN YOU JOIN

  1. Subscribe on Patreon

    Choose Sergeant or Officer tier at patreon.com/c/Regiment1. Your support activates immediately.

  2. Role Assigned in Discord

    Leadership assigns your Patron or Sustainer role. You gain access to the #patreon-supporters channel and all tier benefits.

  3. Personal Welcome from Command

    Within 72 hours, you receive a direct welcome from Warlord. You're not just a subscriber — you're recognized by name.

  4. First Exclusive Content Drop

    Within 14 days, your first patron-exclusive content is delivered — behind-the-scenes updates, mission teasers, or campaign briefings depending on the active cycle.

  5. Officer Tier: Profile Page Built

    Officer-tier Sustainers receive a static profile page on the Regiment website. Your callsign, rank, and contribution are part of the permanent record.
