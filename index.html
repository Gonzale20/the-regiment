// Induction Protocol

New Recruit Onboarding

Welcome to The Regiment. Follow these steps in order to get fully integrated into the unit. Do not skip steps — each one is required before mission clearance.

01
Submit Registration

Fill out the New Member Registration form. This logs you in our system, assigns you to a squad pipeline, and triggers your welcome brief. Takes 3 minutes.

Register Now ↗
02
Join the Discord

All Regiment operations run through Discord. Set your nickname to your gamertag, read the rules in #rules, and introduce yourself in #introductions.

Join Discord ↗
03
Complete Combat Basics Certification

Required before your first mission slot. Covers fireteam movement, comms protocol, weapons handling, and squad-level tactics. Self-paced, accessible in Discord.

View Training Courses
04
Receive Squad Assignment

Based on your registration, leadership will assign you to a squad or confirm community member status. Your squad leader will reach out within 48 hours.

View Active Squads
05
Deploy — First Mission

Sign up for the next Saturday Battle Day in #mission-signup on Discord. Your squad leader will brief you before the operation. Welcome to The Regiment.

View Mission Schedule

Training & Certifications

All certifications are self-paced. Complete the course, submit your form, and command will issue your official Regiment certification within 48 hours.

🪖 ● Required — All Members
Combat Basics

The foundation. Every Regiment operator must complete Combat Basics before their first mission deployment.

  • Fireteam movement & formations
  • Communication protocols
  • Weapons handling & safety
  • Squad-level tactics
  • Medical & casualty procedures
Start Course ↗
📦 ● Role Certification
Logistics Specialist

For operators in the Supplies & Logistics role. Prerequisite: Combat Basics.

  • Resupply operations
  • Vehicle logistics management
  • Base resupply procedures
  • Field inventory tracking
