// Step 04 — Know Your Path
The SMART Rank System

The Regiment uses the SMART promotion model — Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-bound. Rank is no longer given for time served. It is earned by completing specific, tracked tasks across three categories.

Note: Ranks apply to Squad Members only. To request a promotion review, type !rankreview in any Discord channel with MEE6 — this notifies Squad Leadership in #rank-promotions.
📊 The 3 Promotion Categories
🎯 Activity

Attendance in missions and trainings, tracked monthly. Consistency is the baseline.

📚 Education

Completing specific courses — Leadership I, Logistics, Game Master Module, and more.

🤝 Contribution

Tangible impact — recruiting new members, passing peer evaluations, and leading events.

⚔️ Junior Enlisted (E-1 through E-4)
E-1

Private

Entry rank upon squad assignment. Attend 1 mission or training/month. Follow rules, learn movement, comms, and team roles.

→ To reach PFC: 3 weeks active · 2 squad trainings + 2 missions · 60%+ attendance · SL Eval Form + Regiment Feedback Form
E-2

Private First Class (PFC)

3 weeks active as Private. 2 squad trainings, 2 official missions, 60%+ attendance, active in regiment channels, squad cohesion maintained.

→ To reach LCPL: 3 weeks as PFC · 3 squad trainings · 2 missions/PVPs · Point Man or 2IC in 2 missions · Complete Combat Basics · Recruit 1 member
E-3

Lance Corporal (LCPL)

3 weeks as PFC. Act as Point Man or 2IC in 2 missions. Complete Combat Basics Course or pass competency check. Recruit 1 new member.

→ To reach CPL: 30 days as LCPL · FTL in 2 GM/Public missions · 2 Regiment courses · 2 PvP/Major Op events · Co-host 1 squad training · Recruit 2 active members (Recruit Form)
E-4

Corporal (CPL)

30 days as LCPL. FTL in 2 missions. Complete 2 Regiment Courses. 2 recruits who stay active 2+ weeks. Co-host 1 squad training. 2 PvP or Major Op events.

→ To reach SGT: 30 days as CPL · 3 squad trainings last month · FTL or Squad 2IC in 3+ missions · Leadership Basics Course · 5+ total recruits · Mentor 1 Private
🎖️ NCO Corps — E-5 through E-9
E-5

Sergeant (SGT)

30 days as CPL. Lead as FTL or Squad 2IC in 3+ missions. Complete Leadership Basics Course. 5+ total recruits. Mentor 1 Private through onboarding.

→ To reach SSGT: 30 days as SGT · Lead 6+ missions as SL/TL · 9 total recruits · Leadership Course II + Logistics Specialist · 2 NCO meetings in 60 days · Training Tracker
E-6

Staff Sergeant (SSGT)

30 days as SGT. Lead 6+ missions/PVPs as SL or TL. Manage fireteam of 3+ or host 2+ squad trainings/month for 3 months. 9 total recruits. Squad approval (if SL): 70% with 50% response minimum.

→ To reach GYSGT: 45 days as SSGT · Lead 8+ missions as SL or Platoon 2IC · Recon Specialist + GM Module · 12 total recruits · Staff 1 Regiment-wide event · Obtain Regiment responsibility role for 60 days
E-7

Gunnery Sergeant (GYSGT)

45 days as SSGT. Lead 8+ missions or become Drill Instructor or full-time Recruiter. 2 structured platoon trainings. 12 total recruits. Manage a Regiment responsibility role for 60 days.

Roles available: Community Roles · Patreon Roles · Recruiter Form
E-8

Master Sergeant (MSGT)

60 days as GYSGT. Lead 5+ missions/PVP or 12+ public matches. Host major Regiment training. Hold 3 specialist certifications. GM Module (Proficient). Mentor 2 SSGT/GYSGT. 15+ recruits. Lead 1 Regiment project.

Required forms: Training Form · Training Module Form · SL Eval Form (80% squad response required if SL)
E-9

Sergeant Major (SGM)

60 days as MSGT. Lead 12+ missions, 4 PVPs, or 18+ public matches. All basic training courses completed. Promote 2 TLs to SL. 25+ recruits. Manage Discord channel of 40+ squad members + 2 additional channels. 75% squad approval.

OR alternative paths: Recruit 32+ members into your squad · OR Host 20+ squad/platoon training sessions (all documented via Training Form)
🏛️ Leadership Paths
Option 1 — Independent Squad
  • 8–12 committed members ready
  • Minimum rank of CPL or 45 days in Regiment
  • Create squad identity (name & logo)
  • Not active in another squad
  • Complete Leadership Application
Option 2 — Fireteam Leader
  • Already part of a squad
  • Minimum rank of LCPL
  • Recruit 3–5 members (all must complete Recruit Form)
  • Coordinate with your current Squad Leader
  • Run operations consistently with your fireteam

// Step 05 — Choose Your Path
Squad Member vs Community Member

The Regiment welcomes two types of membership. Understanding the difference will help you choose the path that fits your goals and availability.

👥 Community Member
  • Vibrant Community Space — Connect with Arma players in a welcoming environment
  • Flexible Membership — Join voice channels, share media, and engage at your own pace
  • Community Growth — Invite others and expand the network
  • Freedom to Choose — Participate in squads and events at your own discretion
  • No formal rank obligations or attendance requirements
  • Access to general Discord channels and community events
Best for members who want to be part of the community without structured commitments.
⚔️ Squad Member
  • Structured Gameplay — Join a squad for dedicated training, missions, and leadership development
  • Team Collaboration — Work under experienced squad leaders as a cohesive unit
  • Private Squad Channel — Access your squad's dedicated channel for strategy and coordination
  • Leadership Advancement — Progress through ranks and take on leadership roles
  • Role Specialization — Earn specialized roles and certifications
  • Participate in competitive PVP, GM Missions, and major operations
Best for members who want structure, progression, and a dedicated team.
