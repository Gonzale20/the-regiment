Onboarding

Follow the steps exactly. This reduces confusion and gets you mission-ready fast.

Register ↗ Join Discord ↗
Step-by-Step
New members
1) Registration
Complete registration so leadership can route you into the correct pipeline (squad / reserves / PVP path).
Open Form ↗
2) Discord Setup
Set nickname to your gamertag, read rules, introduce yourself.
Join Discord ↗
3) Combat Basics
Mandatory before your first mission slot. This sets standards and prevents chaos in ops.
Start Course ↗
4) Squad Assignment
Leadership assigns you based on availability + role preference. Your squad lead reaches out.
5) Mission Signup + Brief
Sign up in Discord and show up early for briefing. Follow comms discipline.

Ranking System

Reserves (community members) are not ranked. Squad members progress via training, performance, and responsibilities. Update the rank names/requirements here once finalized.

Ranking Doc ↗
How Promotion Works
Simple rules
Ranks are earned, not given
Complete required courses, demonstrate discipline, follow comms, and meet role responsibilities.
Higher ranks require more responsibility
Leadership ranks require reliability, attendance, and the ability to lead under pressure.
Rank maintenance is real
If responsibilities aren’t met, rank can be reviewed and adjusted.

// Discipline & Standards

Rules & SOP

These are the core standards that keep missions clean and squads effective. Keep them short, enforceable, and consistent.

Rules Doc ↗
SOP Library
Expandable
Keep comms short, clear, and relevant. No cross-talk during contact.
  • Use callouts: direction, distance, target type, urgency.
  • Leader comms take priority.
  • Confirm orders with short read-backs.
Orders flow down. Feedback flows up. No freelancing in ops.
  • Squad Lead owns squad execution.
  • Fireteam Lead owns movement + contact handling.
  • Operators execute, report, and support.
If you sign up, show up. If you can’t, notify leadership early.
  • Be on time for briefing.
  • Follow mission ROE and objectives.
  • Keep it respectful—no drama during ops.
Training is how we keep quality high and performance consistent.
  • Complete Combat Basics before first deployment.
  • Role certs are required for role assignments.
  • Leaders must maintain attendance and readiness.
