// Operational Standards
Standards & SOP
The non-negotiables that keep the unit professional. Leadership is expected to know and enforce these. Share with new leaders during onboarding.
Activity Standards
// How we define member status
✅
Active
Seen in game or in Discord within the last 14 days. Eligible for all ops slots and squad roles. Green status.
⚠
At-Risk
No activity for 15–30 days. Squad Leader must make direct contact within 72h. If no response, escalate to Platoon Leader.
❌
Churned / Inactive
No activity for 31+ days with no LOA on file. Removed from active squad. Retained in roster for 30 additional days before archive.
📋
Leave of Absence
Member submitted LOA form with return date. Status paused. No churn flag. Squad Leader checks in on return date. If no-show, moves to At-Risk.
Training Standards
// Non-negotiable execution
📋
Registration Link Posted 3+ Days Prior
The Training Registration Form link must be shared in the appropriate Discord channel at least 3 days before each session. Pinned + announced.
✅
Attendance Logged Within 24 Hours
The Attendance Log form must be submitted by the instructor within 24h of every session. Missing logs are escalated to Command at 48h.
🎯
Combat Basics Before Ops Slots
No member may be placed in an operational slot until Combat Basics is marked Complete in Smartsheet. Instructors are accountable for the mark.
📈
Cert Completion Update
After any certification session, the instructor updates the member's cert status in the Training Sheet within 48h. Completion visible on roster immediately.
Leadership Cadence
// Recurring responsibilities
📅
Weekly: Squad Leader Check-In
Every Squad Leader contacts all At-Risk members weekly. Outcome logged in Smartsheet (contacted / no response / resolved). Escalate no-response to Platoon Leader.
📅
Bi-Weekly: Leadership Sync
All leadership meets bi-weekly. Agenda: health scores, open actions, training schedule, mission pipeline, escalations. Each leader submits Leadership Notes form before the meeting.
📅
Monthly: Roster Audit
Platoon Leaders audit their full roster for accuracy. Churned members archived. Pending members followed up. Certification statuses verified against Smartsheet training records.
Escalation Procedures
// When to escalate and how
🚨
Missing Attendance Log (48h)
If attendance is not logged 48h after a session: Squad Leader escalates to Platoon Leader. Platoon Leader contacts instructor directly. Logs outstanding item in Action Queue.
👥
Member No-Show (72h no response)
At-Risk member does not respond within 72h of Squad Leader contact: Platoon Leader attempts outreach. After 7 days total silence with no LOA: Status moved to Churned.
🚩
Conduct Issue
Any rule violation or conduct concern: Incident Report form submitted immediately. Do not wait. Command reviews within 24h. Matter is confidential until resolved.
